Meet Dan Gigi

A Master of Attractions and Multimedia Complexes

With a wealth of experience, Dan Gigi is a trailblazer, transforming entertainment spaces into extraordinary experiences. At the heart of his achievements lies the prestigious Magic Kass Complex in Ma'ale Adomim.

Magic Kass stands as a beacon of quality and beauty in the indoor gaming world, ranking among the top 9 complexes globally. It has become the heart of the area, drawing visitors from afar with its magnetic appeal.
Overseeing every detail, the Impact company meticulously planned and crafted the 10,000 square meter space. Each element reflects a perfect fusion of expertise and care.
Join us in experiencing the allure of attractions that inspire wonder, innovation that captivates, and a legacy of excellence in indoor gaming and entertainment.
